# The JetBrains family

JetBrains makes a whole family of IDEs that share the same underlying
platform and keybindings. Once you are comfortable in PyCharm, the
others feel familiar:

- **IntelliJ IDEA** — the flagship IDE for Java, Kotlin, and the JVM.
- **WebStorm** — for JavaScript, TypeScript, and front-end work.
- **PhpStorm** — for PHP projects.
- **RubyMine**, **GoLand**, **CLion**, **Rider** — Ruby, Go, C/C++,
  and .NET respectively.
- **DataGrip** — a dedicated SQL and database client.
- **Fleet** — JetBrains' newer, lighter multi-language editor.

Most come in a Community (free) and Professional (paid) edition. If
you want a single IDE that covers many languages,
**IntelliJ IDEA Ultimate** bundles almost all of them.
